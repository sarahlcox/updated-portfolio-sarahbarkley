Home - Sarah Barkley

I'm a mom, photographer, yogi, and dreamer. I have been creating art since I was a child, and have always had a camera by my side. I have to mention that my first camera was an orange Ninja Turtle camera that said, “Cowabunga, smile!” every time I released the shutter. I graduated in May 2012 from Northern Illinois University. I am a 200 hour registered yoga teacher, and a licensed real estate agent. I own my own business, and am on my 10th year! I am inspired by the simplicity of the world around me, and tend to always look on the bright side. I am drawn toward big open fields at dusk. I love the moon. I am passionate about interior design, and always have the itch to rearrange something in my home. I love flea markets, and antique shops. I enjoy getting lost in a book, and I am usually reading something by a great entrepreneur or leader. I love writing, and journaling. I like to hike, do yoga, and run.
